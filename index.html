I have an M.Sc. degree in Cognitive Neuroscience from Sapienza di Roma. I am interested in visual perception (human and machine), particularly face perception. Previously, at Prof.Galati's lab I studied the functional connectivity of the face network in the ventral visual stream at rest.
Currently, at Justus-Liebig-Universität Gießen. in Dr Dobs's lab I am more interested in the computational principles that drive our vision. Thus, I am using Convolutional Neural Networks as a testbed for exploring human face perception and the functional organization of the visual stream.
